About Me

Hi! I'm Julian. I'm currently a 3rd Year Computer Science major at UC Davis. Recently I've tried my hand at Front End Development, and what you see before you is a product of it. To me Front End Development demonstrates the best, tangible, part of Software Engineering: taking an idea and bringing it to life with just a few lines of code. I also have experience doing Back End Development and Project Management, which you can see in the projects I've done and am currently working on.
This website is my attempt at conveying who I am, as both a developer and an individual, and it is my hope that it can help give you an idea of who I am, what I can do, and what I care about, with just a few lines of code.
