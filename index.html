About Me

Hi! I'm Julian. I'm currently a 4th Year Computer Science major at UC Davis. My passion for Computer Science lies in its ability to help make the lives of others better. There are many unexplored applications of computer science, both as a standalone applications in addition to its use as a tool to augment and further other fields like Linguistics or Epidemiology and it is my goal to explore those applications.
