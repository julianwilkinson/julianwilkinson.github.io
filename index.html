About Me

Hi! I'm Julian. I'm currently a 3rd Year Computer Science major at UC Davis. Recently I've tried my hand at Front End Development, and what you see before you is a product of it. To me, front end development demonstrates the best, part of Software Engineering: taking an idea and making it tangible with just a few lines of code. I also have experience with back end development and project management, which you can see in the projects I've done and am working on.

This website is my attempt at conveying who I am, as both a developer and an individual. It is my hope that it can help give you an idea of who I am, what I can do, and what I care about, with just a few lines of code.
